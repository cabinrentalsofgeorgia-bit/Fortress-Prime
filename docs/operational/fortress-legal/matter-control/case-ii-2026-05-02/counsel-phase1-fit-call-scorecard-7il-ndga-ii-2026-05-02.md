## Red Flags

| Red Flag | Stop / Clarify Response |
|---|---|
| Wants the full package before confirming fit and scope | Stop package release; ask for Phase 1 terms first. |
| Treats lack of service as a reason to delay all preparation | Ask how quickly they can act after service; downgrade if vague. |
| Pushes an aggressive counterclaim posture without reviewing elements | Downgrade; require element and risk review. |
| Dismisses counterclaims without looking at the record | Downgrade; ask for reasoned preservation analysis. |
| Wants an unbounded retainer with no Phase 1 deliverables | Stop; ask for capped or milestone-based review. |
| Cannot identify who will do the work | Downgrade; ask for staffing and supervision. |
| Treats the curated record as a problem rather than an efficiency gain | Downgrade; likely expensive restart. |
| Requests privileged communications before engagement terms are clear | Stop and reassess. |
| Cannot commit to a first-response timeline if service occurs | Do not rely on them for P0 posture. |
