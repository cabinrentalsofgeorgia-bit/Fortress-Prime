# Counsel Conflict-Check Outreach Control - 7IL Case II

Date: 2026-05-02
Classification: Repo-safe counsel-outreach control. Not a court filing, not an engagement letter, and not legal advice.
Operator service status: Gary Knight has not been served as of 2026-05-02.

Purpose: keep counsel outreach disciplined. The first contact is for conflict clearance only. Evidence, strategy, draft pleadings, privileged communications, and attorney work product stay withheld until the receiving lawyer or firm clears conflicts and the operator chooses the next release phase.

## Control Rule

No attorney package, evidence index, draft answer, counterclaim theory, inspection-report detail, DOT/GNRR material, or private counsel communication is sent during Phase 0. Phase 0 asks only whether counsel can clear conflicts and consider a short Phase 1 fit call.

## Release Ladder

| Phase | Name | What May Be Sent | What Stays Withheld | Exit Condition |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Conflict screen | Names, case caption, court, case number, service status, request for conflict check. | Facts, evidence, strategy, draft pleadings, privileged communications, attorney package. | Firm says conflicts are clear or asks for limited additional conflict names. |
| 1 | Fit screen | Public posture, deadline/service status, narrow scope ask, budget/fee questions, and scorecard review. | Detailed evidence, privileged analysis, draft answer, counterclaim merits. | Firm passes `counsel-phase1-fit-call-scorecard-7il-ndga-ii-2026-05-02.md` and asks for curated materials. |
| 2 | Confidential package review | Attorney briefing package, selected public filings, curated evidence index, draft workbenches, subject to counsel instructions. | Internal decision logs and privileged counsel communications unless counsel specifically requests them and operator approves. | Phase 1 scope, budget, and confidentiality expectations are acceptable. |
| 3 | Engagement / filing work | Working drafts, filing posture, response calendar, evidence map, and tactical decisions. | Anything outside agreed scope. | Engagement letter or explicit limited-scope agreement is in place. |

## No-Send Triggers

- Recipient has not cleared conflicts.
- Recipient asks for merits, documents, or evidence before conflict clearance.
- Service status or deadline is unknown.
- The communication would reveal attorney advice, private strategy, or privileged email substance.
- The operator has not chosen a Phase 1 budget and acceptable scope.
- The target is being used to test a claim theory instead of to hire counsel.
