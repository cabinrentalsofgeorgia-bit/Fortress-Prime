## Core Test

Do not accept "inspection item = repair obligation." Every alleged repair item must pass this chain:

1. Identified in a source inspection report.
2. Incorporated into PSA/amendment or separately agreed in writing.
3. Not waived, modified, merged, or satisfied at closing.
4. Not outside the Case I judgment/specific-performance boundary.
5. Same condition in 2025, not a new/different/post-closing condition.
6. Causally tied to Knight conduct and recoverable damages.
