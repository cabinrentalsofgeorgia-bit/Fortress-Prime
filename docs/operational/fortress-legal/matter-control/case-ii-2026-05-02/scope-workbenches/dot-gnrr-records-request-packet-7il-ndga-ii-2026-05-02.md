### Draft Subject

Open Records Request - GNRR-0184 Knight Crossing MP386.3 / Georgia Northeastern Railroad / GDOT Lease and Right-of-Way Records

### Draft Body

To the Georgia Department of Transportation Office of Legal Services:

Please treat this as a written Open Records Act request for existing records in GDOT's possession, custody, or control related to the private road grade crossing identified as GNRR-0184 Knight Crossing MP386.3, and the Georgia Northeastern Railroad / GDOT rail corridor in the Blue Ridge to McCaysville area.

Please provide electronic copies where available. If any portion of this request is unclear, please contact the requester before narrowing the search. If responsive records exist but will require time or cost to produce, please provide the statutory response, description of available records, and cost estimate before production.

Requested records:

1. The railroad lease, right-of-way agreement, operating agreement, amendment, renewal, assignment, or successor agreement between Georgia DOT and Georgia Northeastern Railroad Company LLC, or any related operator, referenced as beginning July 17, 1998 and covering railroad-purpose property in Fannin County, Gilmer County, or the Blue Ridge to McCaysville / Copperhill rail segment.
2. Any right-of-way maps, valuation maps, parcel maps, plats, deed records, title memoranda, property descriptions, or corridor maps showing GDOT-owned railroad property or right-of-way at or near Milepost 386.3 / GNRR-0184 / Knight Crossing.
3. Any GDOT crossing file, permit file, license file, approval, correspondence, notice, inspection, maintenance, insurance, indemnity, termination, or assignment record for GNRR-0184 Knight Crossing MP386.3 or any private road grade crossing agreement involving Cabin Rentals of Georgia, LLC, Gary Knight, Lizabeth Knight, Georgia Northeastern Railroad Company LLC, or adjacent Lots 14, 15, or 16 near the crossing.
4. Records identifying the operator, lessee, assignee, license holder, maintenance contact, or control party for the Blue Ridge to McCaysville / Copperhill GNRR segment as of March 17, 2025 and June 2, 2025.
5. Any records reflecting GDOT consent, notice, objection, or no-objection regarding private use, easement use, access rights, or lot-owner/guest use of the GNRR-0184 / MP386.3 crossing.
