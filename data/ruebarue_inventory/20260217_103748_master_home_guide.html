Check-In

Check-in is at 3:30 pm. We ask that you respect this time frame which allows our cleaners to have the house ready for you. Unless you have an approved early check-in, your Door Code will be texted to you at 3:15, along with the WiFi Password.

Request Early Check-In

An early check-in may be available! Our early check-in service is $50 per hour (excluding tax + processing) up to 2 hours prior to 3:30pm. Send a request below and we will let you know if we can accommodate your early arrival.

[EarlyCheckIn]

Request Late Check-Out

We may be able to offer you a late checkout. This service is $50 per hour (excluding tax + processing) up to 2 hours after 10am. Click the button below to send us the request, and we will let you know if we can accommodate it!

Pet Information

Pet Friendly
All pets must be approved by management. Because a home is deemed pet friendly does not mean that pets are allowed without prior approval and appropriate fees. Some of our homes have certain pet restrictions according to breed, age, etc.

Unfortunately we hate to do this, but if a pet is discovered the pet fees, pet deposit, and pet cleaning fee will be doubled.

If your pet has been approved, please follow the rules regarding your pet.

Grocery Stores

Know where the Grocery Stores are before heading to your cabin ~ for liquor stores, stop in Ellijay. Blue Ridge grocery stores carry beer and wine only.

  1. Walmart - as you come into Blue Ridge, at the big intersection (McDonalds), turn LEFT. At next light turn LEFT. Walmart is to the RIGHT.
  2. Ingles - as you come into Blue Ridge, stay on Hwy 515. At the Orvin Lance Connecter intersection where you see CVS and South Bank to your right and Taco Bell on your left, you will turn LEFT. Past the Taco Bell, turn RIGHT into Ingles parking lot.
  3. Food Lion - Turn RIGHT at the Conoco gas station. At the three-way stop sign, turn LEFT and then LEFT again into Food Lion parking lot.

Rebook Direct and Save

We love our repeat guests and can't wait for your return! Use your Loyalty Reward Points to save! Must be applied at time of booking to a new reservation made over the phone.
