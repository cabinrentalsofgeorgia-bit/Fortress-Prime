╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                            KEY DESIGN PATTERNS                                ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

1. 🏛️ HEXAGONAL ARCHITECTURE (Ports & Adapters)
   • Core logic depends on interfaces, not implementations
   • Swap any adapter (SMS, PMS, AI) by changing ONE file
   • Zero vendor lock-in

2. 🌿 STRANGLER FIG PATTERN (Incremental Migration)
   • Pass-through mode: 100% legacy (safe default)
   • Shadow mode: Legacy + AI comparison (validation)
   • Cutover mode: AI handles specific intents (migration)

3. 📦 DOMAIN-DRIVEN DESIGN (Rich Models)
   • Pydantic V2 models with business rules
   • Type-safe, validated, self-documenting

4. 🔍 OBSERVABILITY (Structured Logging)
   • JSON logs with trace_id for correlation
   • Event-based logging (not plain text)
   • Production-ready for log aggregators

╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Week 1:     Pass-through    (ENABLE_AI_REPLIES=false, SHADOW_MODE=false)
Week 2-4:   Shadow          (ENABLE_AI_REPLIES=false, SHADOW_MODE=true)

Week 7-10:  Expand Intents  (Add ACCESS_CODE, CHECKIN, etc.)
Week 11+:   Full AI         (AI_FILTER= empty = all intents)
