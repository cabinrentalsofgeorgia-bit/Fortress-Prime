Three couples - Forever Family and Forever Friends from Atlanta, GA.  We've traveled together for the past 6 years, always to revive our marriages and re-energize our souls.  This year was no different...we laughed together, cried together, and prayed together.  The cabin was beautiful, very spacious, well decorated and provided the privacy that we needed.  We definitely plan to come back, which would be a first for us because each year we try a different cabin.  We even got to see a little snow.  But our next visit will be during the warmer months so that we can enjoy the hot tub, gazebo, fire pit and creek.  It was simply too cold to go outside this time around.  Cannot wait until the next time!  Thank you!
