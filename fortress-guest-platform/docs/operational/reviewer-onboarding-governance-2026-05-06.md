# Fortress Legal Reviewer Onboarding Governance - 2026-05-06

Status: GOVERNED_REVIEWER_ONBOARDING_REQUIRED

## Purpose

Reviewer onboarding ensures every internal pilot reviewer understands operational limits before touching review queues.

## Required Acknowledgments

- Counsel signoff remains pending.
- External submission is not authorized.
- AI outputs are not final legal advice.
- Locked/restricted materials remain metadata-only.
- Unresolved sources remain excluded from relied-upon sections.
- Reviewers do not receive uncontrolled authority.
- Reviewer actions must be auditable and rollback-aware.

## Role Scope

- operator_reviewer: operational triage only.
- source_reviewer: source-chain review and evidence-pending workflow only.
- counsel_or_senior_reviewer: contradiction, legal-interpretation-needed, and high-materiality review only.
- privilege_counsel_metadata_review: metadata-only restricted review only.

## Forbidden Onboarding Outcomes

Onboarding may not grant signoff authority, filing/service/email authority, final legal advice authority, schema/RLS control, ingestion authority, locked-content access, or source-promotion authority.
